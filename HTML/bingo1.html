Bingo (vs Bot mode)

About the game

“I mean suspense, twists are almost impossible these days.” -Danny Boyle
We all like some suspense to spice up our lives. In our humble venture, we present you ‘BINGO (vs BOT)’ where you tackle the AI genius in a game of Bingo ! So put your strategic skills to their best use, to go enjoy this immersive game with our bot. Its fun to play, all you have to do is, choose numbers on your Bingo aheet and strike them out. The aim is to complete striking all the numbers in a rows , columns and diagonals. The winner has to complete five lines in least possible moves.
You need to enter each row of the sudoku with 0s for blank spaces and rest numbers where they occur.
