Bingo (Multiplayer mode)

Steps

1. Install Dropbox from https://www.dropbox.com/install
2. Run the downloaded .exe file of Drop box and continue the setup process.
3. Login with the same Drop box accont in both the player computers.
4. There are two .cpp files , namely player1 and player2. Download these files in differnent computers respectively.
The links are
5. For player1: Open the player1.cpp and go to line number 275 and 287 and enter your Dropbox storage path follwed by \\score.txt.
Eg:C:\\Users\\Karthik\\Dropbox\\score.txt 6. For player2: Open the player2.cpp and go to line number 264 and 275 and enter your Dropbox storage path follwed by \\score.txt. Eg:C:\\Users\\Anubhaw\\Dropbox\\score.txt
7. Both players save the changes and run the file.
8. Toss will be conducted on player1's computer.
9. Enter your name.
10. Press Enter to toss
11. Make your move by entering a number present on your bingo sheet.
12. Wait for the bot to make its move.The selected numbers will be striken out.
13. Enter 99 to see which numbers have been stricken out in a clear manner.
14. The one who completes 5 lines of numbers in the least possible moves wins !
