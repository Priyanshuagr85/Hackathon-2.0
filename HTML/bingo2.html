Bingo (Multiplayer mode)

About the game

Are you up for putting your intelligence and luck to the test ? Well, you can by playing the globally loved traditional game of “BINGO” ! To make your day way lot better, we present you the “BINGO Online Multiplayer” which incorporates ‘Cloud Computing Technology’ in which you can compete with your friends from all around the world. Its fun to play, all you have to do is, choose numbers on your Bingo aheet and strike them out. The aim is to complete striking all the numbers in a rows , columns and diagonals. The winner has to complete five lines in least possible moves.
You need to enter each row of the sudoku with 0s for blank spaces and rest numbers where they occur.
