Bingo (vs Bot mode)

Steps

1. Enter your name.
2. Press Enter to toss
3. Make your move by entering a number present on your bingo sheet.
4. Wait for the bot to make its move.The selected numbers will be striken out.
5. Enter 99 to see which numbers have been stricken out in a clear manner.
6. The one who completes 5 lines of numbers in the least possible moves wins !
